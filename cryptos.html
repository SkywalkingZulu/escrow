WHY AZANIA

There are Many threats to the safety of internet users. This comes mostly in the form of hackers that use many different methods to attack users of the internet and owners of internet sites. Their aim is to either shut down your site/device completely or crack it open to steal valuable data and even money. This problem lead to the creation of Azania Neptune labs. A decentralised lab creates and distriputes encrypted technologies.

HOW AZANIA?

AZANIA uses a new powerful technology or rather way for technologies to interact with each other known as Blockchain This technology creates safety by decentralisingInternet services. This means instead of a site/application being hosted and controlled in one central area to it being completely distributed to all it's users. For example imagine instead of all calls going through your network provider, they all went straight to the person you are calling. No one will be able to hack your calls because instead of hacking one network provider, thieves will have to hack everyone on the system at all times.

SO WHAT AZANIA?

This BLOCKCHAIN Does not neccessarilly make Azanian websites/applications unhackable. It makes hack attempts unrealistic or not feasible. Another good example would be the protection of digital money. If someone wanted to hack your account. They would have to hack the whole system to get into your account. Here at Azania we doubt you have enough money to solicite an attack of that nature.
